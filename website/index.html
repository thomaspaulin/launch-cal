Launch Cal

The Orbital Rocket Launch Calendar*

If you find yourself missing rocket launches because they aren't in your calendar then look no further!

Here you will find a public Google Calendar which lists upcoming rocket launches from a range of launch providers: from ULA, to SpaceX; from JAXA to ESA.

Calendar Formats

If you use Google Calendar you may add the calendar to your existing calendars, if not, you may use this calendar in iCalendar format.

Please note the iCalendar format does not automatically synchronise with new launches.

If you wish to learn more, such as when other calendar formats will be supported, please subscribe to updates below. Your email will be used solely for update purposes and nothing more.
